Georgia Tech Summer Camps

Description

We have opened registration for our computing summer camps at Georgia Tech. We are offering twelve one-week sessions. We have sessions for elementary (rising 4th-6th), middle school(rising 6th - 8th) and high school (rising 9th to college freshmen). We have a camp for high school students who have some prior programming experience in Java to teach them how to create Android apps in Java.

Requirements

  • Rising elementary, middle, or high school student.

How to Apply

See website at coweb.cc.gatech.edu

MakeGamesWithUs Summer Camp

Description

At MakeGamesWithUs we teach high school and college students how to build iPhone games. Our curriculum is used by MIT, UC Berkeley, Carnegie Mellon, and more. Our Summer Academy is a 2 month intensive summer program where students will design, code and ship their own iPhone game!

Requirements

  • At least 13 years old
  • Basic CS knowledge
  • Mac computer running OSX Mavericks
  • Room and board not included in fee
